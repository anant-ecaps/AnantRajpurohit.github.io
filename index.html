Yahoo Finance + Tweet Sentiment to Analyse and Predict bitcoin trend

Y! Finance Bitcoin Trading

Mining, analyzing, and visualizing Bitcoin price changes with data retrieved from Yahoo Finance and sentiment from Bitcoin-related tweets to predict future trends and determine buy/sell signals. Quantifies tweet sentiments using the VADER Sentiment Analyzer and emoji processing, correlating them with Yahoo finance data. Applied and compared machine learning models, including Logistic Regression, KNN, SVM, Random Forest, and Decision Trees to predict buy/sell signals based on sentiment scores. Visualised the distribution of sentiment and the performance comparison of different classifiers.
